About the Book
EMERGE; LIKE A LOTUS IN A POND is a book that tells the story of my murky background that couldn’t deter me from stepping into the limelight and exhibiting my true beauty. In the words of Fela Durotoye, this book will spur you on to defy the many challenges that may seek to bury the great potentials that you carry within.

The Real Value of Wealth
Discover Exclusive Insights on Real Estate, and how to Build Transgenerational Wealth
Order My Book Now
About the Book
This book gives an exposé on the Real Estate sector and the art of wealth building. The Author shares from his wealth of experience as a seasoned Realtor, who has brokered multi-billion naira worth of local and international Real Estate transactions in Nigeria, South Africa, Canada, Brazil and a host of other countries.
The book provides an in-depth analysis of the prospects the real estate sector has in terms of nation building and wealth creation, amidst other functions.
More about the Book
The Real Value of Wealth can be tagged as a ‘Wealth Book’, an expository guide into the pathways to building a sustainable economic future, advancing the frontiers of national development, and eliminating multidimensional poverty, by leveraging on the real estate sector as a viable tool.
  • Glean first-class insight from a seasoned Real Estate Developer: With experience in the real estate sector spanning to a decade, and multibillion naira in local and international sales, the author shares from his wealth of experience as a real estate developer.
  • Discover pathways to Transgenerational Wealth building: In this book, the author, a real estate magnate, shares actionable insights and advice on building long lasting wealth through real estate investment.
  • The Real Sector as a Lifeline for Economic advancement: The author provides an in-depth analysis of the prospects of the Real Estate sector to deliver sustained growth, shared prosperity, provide jobs, reduce poverty and create sustainable wealth.
  • Access the Exclusive Perks the Real Estate Sector offers: The book also advances thoughts on the merits of investment in real estate, specifically its capacity to hedge growing inflation and currency depreciation.
  • An Advisory for Governmental bodies and stakeholders: In a bid to boost the nation for growth and competitiveness, the book highlights key policy reforms.
  • Discover Effective Strategies to fix Housing Deficit in Nigeria: In contributing meaningfully to bridge the gaping housing deficit in the country, the author pens housing reforms and innovative strategies that would help ensure that more Nigerians can own a home of their own.
  • Access Exclusive Predictions and Projections for the Future: The Future is now! This book reinstates the conviction that the real estate sector can serve as the pathway to building a sustainable economic future.
