EMERGE! - Like a Lotus In The Pond
Defy the odds and achieve your greatest dreams
Order My Book Now
About the Book
EMERGE; LIKE A LOTUS IN A POND is a book that tells the story of my murky background that couldn’t deter me from stepping into the limelight and exhibiting my true beauty. In the words of Fela Durotoye, this book will spur you on to defy the many challenges that may seek to bury the great potentials that you carry within.
Comments on the Book
… Like the lotus, I wish that we all find that dream that inspires us enough to reach for it regardless of whatever overwhelms us and seeks to submerge our potential. Keep growing until you emerge! - Fela Durotoye
  • Break free from being a victim of circumstance:This book spotlights real life scenarios of my limiting background and how I broke free from the harsh realities of hardship to have my own success story.
  • Swim against the tides: If you’ve had your fair share of life’s hardship, rather than settle for a life of misery and abject poverty, this book will help you paddle furiously and stay above the tides.
  • Be Limitless: You’ll get a firsthand experience of how a disadvantaged background is not powerful enough to offset your dreams
  • Achieve Excellence inspite of the Odds: Life might have presented you with 1001 reasons to settle for less than you deserve, this book will ignite you to strive for excellence.
  • Reach for the Light: Emerge is a power-charged book that will drive you to reach for the proverbial light at the end of the tunnel no matter how bleak your circumstances are.
  • Dream beyond Dreams: This book provides a true life account of how to dare greatly and soar higher than you had ever imagined
  • Keep Growing till you Emerge: People do not celebrate efforts, or excuses, people only celebrate results!
